Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

At WildWatchmen, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the personal information you provide while interacting with our wildfire prediction platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you contact us, sign up for updates, or submit location-based queries, we may collect your name, email address, and location data.
  • Non-Personal Information: We automatically collect data such as IP address, device type, browser version, pages visited, and referring URLs through web analytics tools.
  • Location Data: If enabled, we may access your approximate location to provide more relevant wildfire predictions for your area.

2. Use of Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide wildfire trend analysis and risk predictions
  • Improve system accuracy and enhance your user experience
  • Respond to inquiries or feedback
  • Monitor and evaluate platform usage for academic or development purposes

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with educational institutions or emergency response agencies to support research and public safety initiatives.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to analyze user behavior, manage sessions, and personalize content. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we encourage responsible usage.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Requests can be made by contacting us at support@wildwatchmen.com.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to external platforms or tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

8. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. We encourage users to review it regularly. Significant changes will be clearly communicated via our homepage or email notifications.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, feedback, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@wildwatchmen.com.
